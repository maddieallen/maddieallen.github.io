How to Help

Organizations to Donate to:

There are many organizations around the world dedicated to cleaning our oceans. Even if you are unable to volunteer, any donation would be greatly appreciated. Some clean-ocean organizations who accept and appreciate your donations are:
- The Ocean Cleanup
- Ocean Conservancy
- The Environmental Defense Fund
- Oceana

Volunteer Opportunities:

Many organizations hold community volunteer days where you can help actively clean our beaches. For example, organizations such as The Surfrider Foundation and 4Ocean both hold volunteer days to clean up beaches. The best way to find these opportunities is to visit the website of the foundation you want to volunteer with. Also, you do not need to be with an organization to clean up your beaches, you can take time to help whenever you please.

Sustainable Brands to Purchase From:

There are many brands that provide an alternative to plastic products and also donate proceeds to help our oceans. For example, the brand 4Ocean (previously mentioned) sells bracelets and other accessories made from recycled plastics and glasses found in our oceans and on our beaches. Another sustainable brand you can purchase from is Fanatics, which is an apparel company that donates a portion of their proceeds to help save sharks, major components of the ocean ecosystem. By purchasing from these sustainable brands, you are not only reducing the amount of plastic you use, by also giving to organizations that will take a step towards cleaner oceans.

How to Spread the Word:

Although simply spreading the word about the issue of marine plastic pollution seems futile, it is very important. The more people who are aware of this growing problem the greater the possibility of action becomes. You can spread the word by talking to your friends, posting information about it on social media, and wearing informative products from sustainable brands, and any other way you would normally spread information.
